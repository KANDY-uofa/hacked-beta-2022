About Project

Goal: Our goal was to raise awareness on road safety, specifically in Alberta.

Traffic Graph

This interactive graph shows number of car crash by years. The data was not accessible as it seems. Data was scattered on the internet as a piece of information. Our team has used web scrapping skills to collected data to demonstrate how data scrapping is done. How they are actually useful. Data is just a piece of word before it is processed to become something useful. We believe that this kinds of skill can be done by group of students and the data should be introduced to someone who might actually need this data.
